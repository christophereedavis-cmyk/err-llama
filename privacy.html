Errllama
Privacy Policy

Errllama is a self-hosted AI chat service operated by Errldabbin. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

What We Collect

  • Username — chosen by you at registration
  • Password — stored as a one-way hash, never in plain text
  • Chat history — your conversations with Errllama, stored privately on our server
  • Timestamps — when conversations were created and updated

We do not collect email addresses, real names, payment information, or any identifying information beyond your chosen username.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the Errllama chat service
  • To save and display your conversation history
  • To authenticate you when you log in

Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Where Your Data Lives

All data is stored in a private database on a self-hosted server. Conversations are processed locally by an AI model running on the same server — your messages are never sent to OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or any external AI provider.

Web traffic is routed through Cloudflare for security and performance. Cloudflare may log connection metadata such as IP addresses per their own privacy policy.

Data Retention

Your account and conversation history are retained until you delete them or request deletion. You can delete individual conversations from within the app at any time.

Your Rights

  • You can delete any conversation at any time from within the app
  • You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data
  • You can request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, use the contact form.

Security

Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before storage. All connections are encrypted via HTTPS. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, though no system is perfectly secure.

Children

Errllama is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date below. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or requests regarding your data can be submitted via the contact form.
